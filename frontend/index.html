📊 Confidence Score Calculation

Statistical Basis: Based on 91% prediction intervals from quantile regression models (1st-99th percentile).

How it works:

  • Narrow intervals = Higher confidence (model is certain)
  • Wide intervals = Lower confidence (model is uncertain)

Mathematical Formula:

interval_width = |upper_bound − lower_bound| confidence = max(0, min(1, 1 − interval_width))

Interpretation:

  • 85-100%: Very reliable prediction
  • 75-84%: Reliable prediction
  • 65-74%: Moderate reliability
  • <65%: Less reliable, consider additional factors

Note: Higher confidence doesn't change the risk prediction, only indicates how certain the model is about that prediction.
